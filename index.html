Sunset Tour

The Sunset Tour is a romantic and relaxing experience created for couples who want to enjoy the Algarve coast at the most beautiful time of the day. It combines the charm of a private boat trip with the magic of the setting sun over the Atlantic.

The journey begins at the Ferragudo pier, sailing calmly along the Arade River towards the ocean. Along the way, you will pass riverside beaches, fishing areas, marinas and the historic Castle of Ferragudo before reaching the open sea. From there, we follow the coastline past Praia da Rocha and Praia do Vau, heading towards the striking rock formations of João de Arens.

We anchor between the cliffs and crystal-clear waters so you can enjoy the sea at your own pace. There is time to swim, paddleboard, kayak, use our snorkeling gear or simply relax on deck, listening to music, sipping a glass of wine and watching the light soften over the horizon.

As the afternoon fades, we sail back to the mouth of the Arade River, where the boat rests gently on the water while the sun sets behind the cliffs. It is a calm and breathtaking moment, with the sky painted in warm shades of gold and pink.

The Sunset Tour is more than a boat trip. It is a slow and beautiful celebration of the sea, the light and the shared silence of the evening.

Custom Tour

The Custom Tour is an exclusive and private experience designed for couples who want complete freedom to create their perfect day at sea along the stunning Algarve coast. With an approximate duration of 6 hours and 30 minutes, this tour allows you to decide what to do and where to go, making every moment unique.

You may choose to sail to the famous Benagil Cave, explore the dramatic coastline near Carvoeiro, Lagoa, Portimão, Lagos or Alvor, or anchor at a secluded beach accessible only by boat, where you can enjoy the rare feeling of having an entire beach just for the two of you.

During the tour, there is plenty of time for activities such as swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, paddleboarding (SUP), or simply floating together on the water. You can relax under the sun, listen to music, share a glass of wine, and enjoy the calm and privacy of the ocean.

We can also raise the sails and head farther from the coast to feel the stronger ocean breeze and the freedom of open water. Your captain can share a few sailing lessons if you wish, turning the experience into a playful and interactive adventure.

You may choose to stop at remote beaches for a picnic, explore hidden coves, or simply stay anchored to soak up the sun and enjoy each other’s company. Personalized souvenirs and small gifts are provided on board to make the day even more memorable.

Every detail of the Custom Tour is flexible and personal, created to match your mood, your pace, and your idea of a perfect day together along the beautiful Algarve coast.

Amor Tour

The Amor Tour is a gentle and romantic escape created for couples who want to enjoy the beauty of the Algarve coast in a calm and private way. Departing from Ferragudo pier at 10:00 AM or 2:00 PM, it offers the perfect balance between relaxation, discovery and connection.

The journey begins along the Arade River, passing fishing villages, riverside beaches, marinas and the historic Castle of Ferragudo before reaching the ocean. From there, we sail towards the breathtaking rock formations of João de Arens, gliding past Praia da Rocha and Praia do Vau, where dolphins are sometimes seen playing near the bow.

We anchor between cliffs and turquoise waters for a moment to enjoy the sea. You can swim, paddleboard, kayak, use our snorkeling gear or simply float together under the sun, listening to music, sharing a glass of wine and taking in the scenery.

Throughout the experience, your guide reveals stories about the region, the shapes of the rocks and the marine life that inhabits these waters.

As the tour draws to a close, we turn back toward Ferragudo and raise the sails, letting the western wind carry us home. It is a quiet and beautiful part of the journey, where the captain may share a few simple sailing tips with those who wish to learn.

Before you disembark, a few personalised gifts will be waiting on board, a small reminder of the time you shared at sea.

The Amor Tour is not just a boat trip. It is a slow and graceful experience made to be remembered.
